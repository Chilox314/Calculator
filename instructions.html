Instructions:

This Calculator works from left to right.
Operator order: !, √, then from left to right.
No support for signs yet.

Errorcauses:

    • Factorials with a floating point
      Dividing by zero
      Two floating points in one number
      Two operators following each other
      x!y or x√y can lead to unwanted results due to missing operators.
